Vuelta Replica

The framesets are a tribute to Remco Evenepoel's incredible achievements and to our continued commitment to innovation and perfection. Remco's lucky number is 13, the number with which Remco won the Junior World Championships and Liège-Bastogne-Liège. All 13 framesets are hand-painted at Color Monkey in Geraardsbergen and are numbered by frame. These framesets are identical to the frame used by Remco Evenepoel during the Vuelta.
