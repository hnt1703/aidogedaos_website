Frequently asked questions
01
01
How long does it take to spray paint a bike?

This depends on the specifications and complexity of your needs, but it usually takes between 4 and 6 weeks. During our introductory meeting, we delve deeper into the planning.

02
02
Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes, we offer a 1-year warranty on all our repairs.

03
03
Can you spray any type/brand of bike?

Yes, we can spray and personalize bicycles from all brands. For Specialized bicycles, this is even subject to the original manufacturer's warranty. In consultation, this is also possible for Canyon Belgium bicycles.

04
04
Can I send my bike for repair or painting?

Yes, you can send us your bike or frame. Please contact us first for the correct shipping instructions and packaging advice.
